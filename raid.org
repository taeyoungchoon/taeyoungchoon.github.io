* kinds of

| type   | desc                 |
|--------+----------------------|
| raid 0 | stripe               |
| raid 1 | mirror               |
| raid 5 | at least three disks |

* block not file

* raid 0, stripe

| disk   | block |
|--------+-------|
| disk 1 | a     |
| disk 2 | b     |

* raid 1, mirror

| disk   | block |
|--------+-------|
| disk 1 | a,b   |
| disk 2 | a,b   |

* raid 5

| disk   | block |
|--------+-------|
| disk 1 | a,c   |
| disk 2 | a,b   |
| disk 3 | b,c   |
